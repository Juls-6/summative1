ABOUT THE CAFE

Green Gable's Café is a Japanese-Inspired Coffee Shop located at the clubhouse of La Finca Farm and Country Resort in Lipa City.The café is small and has an al fresco seating in the garden near the pool.

Impressive drinks menu at Green Gable's Café, featuring a wide selection of coffees, teas, and refreshing slush drinks.You can also enjoy an array of delectable pastries to complement your beverage at Green Gable's Café.

"Green Gable's Cafe is not only known for its delicious food but also for its exceptional coffee. Take the time to sample their specialty coffees." Fresh is the new sweet.

Chef, Coffeeist and Owner: Julius Anonuevo

Opening hours: everyday from 6am to 5pm.

Address: San Pablo Road, Brgy. Dagatan, Lipa City, Philippines
